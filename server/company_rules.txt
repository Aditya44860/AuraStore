AuraStore India - Company Policies & Rules

1. Shipping & Delivery
- We offer free standard shipping on all orders over ₹2,999.
- For orders under ₹2,999, a flat shipping fee of ₹150 applies.
- Delivery Timelines:
  - Metro Cities (Mumbai, Delhi, Bangalore, etc.): 2-4 business days.
  - rest of India: 5-7 business days.
- Express delivery (Next Day) is available in select metros for ₹499.
- We currently ship to all valid PIN codes across India via our logistics partners.

2. Returns & Exchanges
- You can initiate a return or exchange within 15 days of delivery.
- Items must be in original condition with all tags, packaging, and invoices intact.
- Reverse Pickups: We offer free reverse pickup for return requests.
- Refunds:
  - Prepaid orders: Refunded to original payment method within 5-7 business days.
  - COD orders: Refunded as Store Credit or Bank Transfer.
- Innerwear and items marked "Non-Returnable" cannot be returned for hygiene reasons.

3. Privacy & Data Protection
- We comply with Indian Data Protection regulations.
- All payment transactions are secured via PCI-DSS compliant gateways.
- We do not share your mobile number or email with third-party telemarketers.

- Email: support.in@aurastore.com

5. Payment Methods
- UPI: Google Pay, PhonePe, Paytm, and BHIM.
- Cards: All major Indian Debit/Credit cards (Visa, MasterCard, RuPay).
- Net Banking: Support for 50+ Indian banks.
- Cash on Delivery (COD): Available for orders up to ₹10,000.

6. Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)
- Q: Do you ship internationally?
  - A: Currently, AuraStore India only ships within the domestic borders of India.
- Q: Can I pay via EMI?
  - A: Yes, we offer No-Cost EMI on select credit cards for orders above ₹5,000.
- Q: How do I track my shipment?
  - A: You will receive a WhatsApp and Email notification with a tracking link once your order is dispatched.

7. Aura Rewards India
- Earn 5 Aura Points for every ₹100 spent.
- 100 Points = ₹10 discount on future purchases.
- Special birthday discounts for Silver and Gold tier members.
