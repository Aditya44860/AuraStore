AuraStore - Company Policies & Rules

1. Shipping & Delivery
- We offer free shipping on all orders over $100.
- For orders under $100, a flat shipping fee of $10 applies.
- Standard delivery takes 3-5 business days. 
- Express delivery is available for $25 and takes 1-2 business days.
- We currently ship to all states in the US and Canada.

2. Returns & Exchanges
- You can return or exchange any item within 30 days of purchase.
- Items must be in their original condition, unworn, and with all tags attached.
- Returns for store credit are free.
- Returns for a refund to the original payment method incur a $5 restocking fee.
- Sale items marked as "Final Sale" cannot be returned or exchanged.

3. Privacy Policy
- We value your privacy and protect your personal data.
- We use industry-standard encryption for all transactions.
- We do not sell your personal information to third parties.
- Cookies are used only to improve your shopping experience and remember your cart.

5. Payment Methods
- We accept all major credit cards (Visa, MasterCard, American Express).
- We also support PayPal, Apple Pay, and Google Pay.
- Cryptocurrencies (Bitcoin, Ethereum) are accepted via BitPay.

6. Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)
- Q: Can I track my order?
  A: Yes, once your order is shipped, you will receive a tracking number via email.
- Q: Do you have a physical store?
  A: Currently, AuraStore is an online-only boutique.
- Q: How do I change my account password?
  A: You can change your password in the Profile section after logging in.

7. Membership & Aura Rewards
- Join our Aura Rewards program to earn 1 point for every $1 spent.
- 100 points can be redeemed for a $10 discount on your next purchase.
- Members get early access to sales and exclusive product drops.
